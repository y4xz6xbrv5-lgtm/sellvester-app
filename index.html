Frequently Asked Questions

Why choose annual billing?

Save 20% compared to monthly payments! Annual billing locks in your rate for 12 months and includes 2 months free. Perfect for growing businesses who want the best value.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect immediately. You can also switch between monthly and annual billing.

What happens after the free trial?

You're only charged if you continue. Cancel anytime during the trial with no charges. The 14-day trial applies to Silver and Gold plans.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans, whether monthly or annual.

What is the VIP program builder?

Gold and Platinum vendors can create their own paid customer membership programs (e.g., $20/month with monthly free product). You set the pricing and benefits, and keep the subscription revenue.
